*Easter Egg - Christmas 2006*

Saw this off "Boing Boing (Boing Boing)":http://www.boingboing.net/2006/12/12/flickrs_excellent_xm.html. If you post a Note on your Flickr Photo, it will show you the corresponding object - hat, beard. Find the Easter Egg; type the following words in the Note (without the quotes)

* "ho ho ho hat" -- draws a Santa-hat on your pic
* "ho ho ho beard" -- draws a snowy white beard.

*Christmas Gift from Flickr*

You can purchase a special activation code that you can give to anyone, whether or not they have an existing Flickr account. Flickr have created a "special Gift Certificate (Flickr Christmas Gift)":http://www.flickr.com/gift/ card that you can print out yourself, fold up and stuff in a stocking, under a tree or hidden away for after the candles are lit (of course, you can also send the gift code in an email).
